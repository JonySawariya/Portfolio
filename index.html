BookBuddy

BookBuddy is a comprehensive library management system developed as a college project by a two-member team. The project was designed to streamline and enhance the management of library resources, providing a user-friendly platform for both librarians and users. Leveraging a robust technology stack that includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP, and MySQL, BookBuddy offers features such as book cataloging, member management, and lending/return tracking. The project highlights our ability to design and implement a full-stack web application, focusing on both the back-end functionality and front-end user experience.
